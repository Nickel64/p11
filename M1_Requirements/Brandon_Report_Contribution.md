## 3.3 Usability Requirements

The usability of the final product should meet the both the requirements & objectives 
expected of the client but also the usability needs of all stakeholders. 
Theses usability requirements & objectives can be splits up into three different 
criteria measurable effectiveness, efficiency and satisfaction criteria based in 
context of use of bus factor program.

### Measurable effectiveness

The quality of the program in use from the stakeholder perspective can be
defined on how effective the program is at performing it’s intended task.
Hence for the project to meet usability the requirements & objectives the
product it will need to preform it’s intended task effectively.

* •	Required at least a 90% accuracy detection rate on buses passing red lights.
If the product didn’t have good level of accuracy the product would appear broken 
or un-usable from a stakeholder perspective.
* •	Objective of number plate detection should near perfect accuracy
to avoid false reports of vehicles that didn’t cross red lights.
* •	Video moments generation of when buses cross reds light should be near perfect
 as flagging of a buses crossing a red light occurs. Else the product would appear
 buggy to stakeholders and or end users.
